System 1

Instant Acknowledgement

The Problem

A homeowner fills out your form at 7pm Tuesday. You call next morning. Your competitor called at 7:04pm the same night. The job was gone before you even knew about it.

The fix: The moment a lead comes in, an automated acknowledgement fires. Day or night, weekday or weekend. Your company is first in their inbox before anyone else moves.

02
System 2

Pre-Appointment Alignment

The Problem

Homeowner books. You prepare. They cancel the morning of, or show up skeptical after talking to a neighbor and watching YouTube videos about contractor scams.

The fix: Between booking and your rep showing up, a timed sequence builds trust, handles objections early, and makes sure both decision-makers are in the room. Your rep walks into a warm conversation, not a cold pitch.

03
System 3

Post-Estimate Follow-Up

The Problem

You spend an hour on the job. Write a thorough estimate. Send it over. Silence. Two calls. Nothing. The lead gets filed under "not interested" and forgotten.

The fix: An automated sequence activates after every estimate. It stays in touch, addresses hesitation, and keeps your proposal top of mind until they sign or clearly say no.

04
System 4

Recovery Engine

The Problem

Every home service company has that folder. "Dead leads." "Old quotes." "Not interested." That folder is not dead. It's just untouched revenue waiting for someone to care enough to check in.

The fix: A genuine check-in sequence goes to every lead 28+ days old and every past customer. No sales pitch. Just a natural conversation starter. Every reply routes somewhere useful.

→Past customer happy? We ask for a Google review.
→Past customer needs work? Appointment booked.
→Old lead still interested? New appointment booked.
→Quote expired 40-60 days ago? Fresh estimate booked.

Frequently Asked Questions

FAQs

What exactly is Lead Bridge Systems?

Lead Bridge Systems is a done-for-you automated follow-up service for home service and trades companies. We install four systems into your business that handle everything from the first lead response to recovering contacts that went quiet months ago. You don't touch the tech — we build it, configure it to your business, and keep it running.

Why are my roofing estimates not converting?

The most common reason is the gap between sending the estimate and following up. Industry data shows the average roofing company closes only 20% of estimates. Most leads don't say no — they go quiet because life got busy and nobody followed up at the right time. A structured automated sequence that touches leads at days 2, 5 and 12 after an estimate recovers 15-20% of jobs that would have gone cold.

How do I recover dead roofing leads?

The most effective approach is a genuine check-in — not a sales pitch. Leads that went silent after 28 days respond well to a simple message asking if they ever got the work done. Past customers respond to a check-in asking how the roof is holding up. Our Recovery Engine automates these sequences and routes every reply to a useful outcome: a new booking, a review request, or a clean close.

Does this work for HVAC and plumbing too?

Yes. Lead Bridge Systems was built on three years of roofing industry experience but the four systems work for any home service or trades company that runs estimates — roofing, HVAC, plumbing, pool companies, solar, windows and siding, and restoration. If your business sends estimates and follows up with leads, this works for you.

How long does setup take?

We allow 3-4 weeks. This covers your phone number configuration, email domain warmup (which takes 2-3 weeks on its own), CRM integration if applicable, and full end-to-end testing before any real leads touch the system. We build it right the first time so nothing breaks in month two.

Do I need any technical knowledge?

None at all. Done-for-you means exactly that. We handle every technical aspect — the automation platform, your sending domain, phone number setup, message templates and CRM connection. Your only job during setup is to add three DNS records to your domain (we give you the exact values to paste in) and approve the message copy. After that, it runs on its own.
